# Careers in Cyber

**Path:** Pre Security  
**Platform:** TryHackMe  
**Date completed:** July 14, 2026

## What I learned
- Overview of the different career paths in cybersecurity (offensive, 
  defensive, GRC, etc.)
- Got a clearer picture of roles like penetration tester, SOC analyst, 
  security engineer, and where they fit

## Key takeaway
Cybersecurity isn't one job — it's a whole field with different paths 
depending on whether you enjoy breaking things, defending things, or 
managing risk/policy.
